# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Golfers, coaches, and motivated practice partners reviewing a swing video immediately after capture. They are looking for a fast read on posture, sequencing, balance, and drills without needing to interpret raw pose data.

## Product Purpose

SwingGrade turns an uploaded or recorded golf swing into an actionable analysis: phase checkpoints, pose overlays, category scores, detected swing faults, and drills. Success means the user understands what happened in the swing and leaves with one or two concrete practice priorities.

## Brand Personality

Precise, composed, encouraging. The interface should feel like a trusted analysis station: technical enough to be credible, calm enough to use between swings, and direct enough to support practice decisions.

## Anti-references

Avoid sports-betting dashboards, neon fitness trackers, generic SaaS hero styling, gamified scoreboards, cluttered coaching PDFs, and decorative visuals that distract from the video frame and movement feedback.

## Design Principles

- Put the video and movement evidence before explanation.
- Make scoring legible, but keep drills and next actions more important than the number.
- Use calm confidence: compact structure, measured color, direct copy.
- Reveal complexity progressively: upload first, then phase timeline, then detailed checkpoint cards.
- Treat pose confidence and 2D-video limitations honestly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, touch targets of at least 44px, reduced-motion support, and copy that stays understandable for non-technical golfers.
